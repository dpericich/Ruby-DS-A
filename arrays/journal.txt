2/20/21 - Picking Numbers (HackerRank)

Assumptions:

1) The only argument will be an array of integers
2) We assume that the array is not yet sorted
3) We assume that we are able to sort the integers
4) There will be between 2 and 100 items in the array, inclusive
5) There will be at least one pair (2 items) that meet the criteria

Time Complexity - O (N) || Space Complexity - O (1)
